# StratIQ Studio

 Market Intelligence Platform

StratIQ Studio is a web-based platform that delivers comprehensive market entry analysis — combining real-world economic data with advanced analytical frameworks to support strategic decision-making.

---

## Overview

StratIQ Studio enables analysts and consultants to evaluate market opportunities through a structured, data-driven approach:

- **Real Data Layer** — Economic indicators sourced from the World Bank
- **Analytics Engine** — Strategic frameworks including SWOT, risk assessment, and market scoring
- **Executive Reporting** — Actionable briefs ready for boardroom presentation

---

## Key Features

- Real-time economic indicators (GDP, inflation, trade metrics)
- Market attractiveness scoring system
- SWOT analysis with supporting data
- Interactive visualizations and dashboards
- Risk matrix with mitigation strategies
- Strategic timeline and entry roadmap
- Secure and web-accessible from any device

---

## How It Works

1. Select your parameters:
   - Industry / Sector
   - Target Region
   - Company Type
   - Investment Horizon

2. The platform:
   - Fetches live economic data
   - Processes indicators into structured datasets
   - Generates a comprehensive strategic brief

3. Output includes:
   - Market opportunity score
   - Executive summary
   - Competitive landscape
   - Risk register
   - Strategic recommendations
